Buying eBells

eBells are available to order and will normally be dispatched within two weeks of payment. However eBells are time-consuming to manufacture, and we are a not-for-profit cottage industry, so please be patient. We will advise you of the date of dispatch, and any delays.

Ordering eBells
£49.50 per eBell plus £8.00 postage & packing per UK order

To place an order click on the button below to send an email stating the quantity of eBells, alternative cable connectors (see below) if required, and the delivery name & address. We will confirm the price on receipt of your order and provide details of the Bank Account for you to transfer your payment.

Email your order to sales@ebells.co.uk
